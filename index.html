    wMusic Overview.

    wMusic is a system which allow your RaspberryPi, to stream music from Spotify directly into your hi-fi system. If you have 2 RaspberryPi, one can act like a bridge and the other one (plug on your hi-fi system) can act like a receiver. It's like the Sonos system but for free ;) !
