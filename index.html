    wMusic Overview.

    wMusic is a system which allow your RaspberryPi, to stream music from Spotify directly into your Hi-Fi system !

    Sonos-Like system

    If you have 2 RaspberryPi, you can do your own sonos-like system ! Simply plug one of your RaspberryPi behind your Box, the other on your Hi-Fi system and it's done !
